About Us

KUNGIYA HADINKAN HAISA DA HAISAWAN DUNIYA CHARITY FOUNDATION is a pioneering organization dedicated to fostering unity among the Hausa people and strengthening the bond between Hausa communities and their leaders worldwide. Our mission is rooted in promoting solidarity, cultural pride, and global recognition of the Hausa tribe while addressing critical social issues affecting our people. We work closely with Hausa chiefs and communities to:

  • Reunite and assist Hausa individuals struggling or begging on the streets in foreign countries to return home with dignity.
  • Enhance the global visibility of the rich Hausa culture and heritage.
  • Provide support to the poor and needy through strategic empowerment initiatives.
  • Care for orphans by offering shelter, education, and opportunities for a better future.

At KUNGIYA HADINKAN HAISA DA HAISAWAN DUNIYA, we believe that together, we can create a stronger, more united Hausa tribe and uplift every individual in need. By celebrating our identity and extending a helping hand, we aim to make the Hausa people a symbol of resilience, compassion, and excellence across the globe.

Join us as we build a brighter future for the Hausa people, wherever they may be.

Our Mission

At KUNGIYA HADINKAN HAISA DA HAISAWAN DUNIYA CHARITY FOUNDATION, our mission is to unite, uplift, and empower the Hausa people across the world. We aim to restore dignity to our brothers and sisters living in hardship, especially those begging in foreign lands, by helping them return home and rebuild their lives. We work to promote global awareness of the Hausa identity, preserve our cultural heritage, and support the most vulnerable — including the poor, the needy, and orphans. Through strong collaboration with Hausa chiefs and community leaders, we are building a future where every Hausa person can thrive with pride, purpose, and peace.

Our Vision

Our vision at KUNGIYA HADINKAN HAISA DA HAISAWAN DUNIYA CHARITY FOUNDATION HADINKAN HAISA DA HAISAWAN DUNIYA is to see a united, respected, and empowered Hausa community that thrives both locally and globally. We envision a future where Hausa culture is recognized and celebrated worldwide, where no Hausa person is left behind, and where the values of compassion, unity, and dignity define our people. We strive to create a strong network of Hausa leaders, youth, and supporters working together to eliminate poverty, restore hope, and build a legacy of pride and progress for generations to come.

Manufarmu

A KUNGIYA HADINKAN HAISA DA HAISAWAN DUNIYA manufarmu ita ce haɗa kan Hausawa, tallafa musu, da kuma ƙarfafa rayuwar al'umma a ko'ina suke a duniya. Muna da niyyar dawo da martaba da mutuncin 'yan'uwanmu da ke cikin wahala, musamman waɗanda ke yin bara a ƙasashen waje, ta hanyar taimaka musu komawa gida da farfado da rayuwarsu. Muna ƙoƙarin wayar da kai a duniya game da asalin Hausawa, kare al'adunmu, da tallafa wa marasa galihu da marayu. Tare da haɗin gwiwar sarakunan Hausa da shugabannin al'umma, muna gina makoma mai kyau inda kowane ɗan Hausa zai rayu cikin alfahari, nufi da salama.

Burinmu

Burinmu  KUNGIYA HADINKAN HAISA DA HAISAWAN DUNIYA shi ne ganin al'ummar Hausawa sun zama tsintsiya madaurinki ɗaya — masu ƙarfi, ɗaukaka, da cikakken ci gaba a gida da wajen ƙasa. Muna fatan ganin al'adun Hausawa sun samu girmamawa a duniya baki ɗaya, inda babu wani ɗan Hausa da zai rage a baya, kuma inda ƙauna, haɗin kai, da mutunci suka zama ginshiƙan rayuwarmu. Muna aiki don gina ƙungiya mai ƙarfi da ke haɗa shugabanni, matasa da masu kishin ci gaban Hausawa don kawar da talauci, dawo da fata, da kuma gina gado mai alfahari da ci gaba ga zuri'a mai zuwa.
